Branch - Colombo

Winfield Hospital - Colombo Branch is the main hub of our healthcare network in Sri Lanka, offering world-class medical services in the heart of the city. As the flagship hospital, it is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and provides comprehensive care across all specialties. Our Colombo branch is renowned for its 24/7 emergency services, ensuring that patients receive critical care at any time of day. With a dedicated team of expert doctors, nurses, and support staff, Winfield Hospital - Colombo is committed to delivering exceptional healthcare with compassion and efficiency.

Negombo - Branch

Winfield Hospital - Negombo Branch is dedicated to offering a wide range of healthcare services tailored to meet the needs of the community. Specializing in urgent care, this branch provides quick and efficient medical attention for non-life-threatening emergencies. In addition to urgent care, the hospital hosts specialty clinics where patients can consult with experienced specialists across various fields. The Negombo branch also offers comprehensive outpatient services, ensuring that patients receive quality medical care without the need for hospitalization. With a focus on accessibility and expertise, Winfield Hospital - Negombo is a trusted healthcare provider in the region.

Kandy - Branch

Winfield Hospital - Kandy Branch is a trusted healthcare provider specializing in maternity, pediatrics, and elderly care. This branch is dedicated to supporting families at every stage of life, offering exceptional maternity services for expectant mothers, including prenatal care and safe delivery. The pediatrics department provides comprehensive care for children, ensuring their health and well-being from infancy through adolescence. Additionally, the Kandy branch is committed to elderly care, with specialized services designed to meet the unique needs of senior patients. Winfield Hospital - Kandy combines compassionate care with medical excellence to serve the community.
